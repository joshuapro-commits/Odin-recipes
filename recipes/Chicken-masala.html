Chicken-masala

Description:

A creamy, spiced curry with grilled chicken in a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast/thighs (cubed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup curee
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices (30 min).
  2. Grill or pan-fry until lightly charred.
  3. In a pan, cook garlic and tomato puree, add cream and chicken.
  4. Simmer 10 min, garnish with coriander. Serve with rice or naan.
