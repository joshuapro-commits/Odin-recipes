Description

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish that starts with sheets of lasagna noodles, layered with rich meat sauce or marinara,

creamy ricotta or béchamel, and plenty of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

The layers are repeated, baked in the oven, and finished with a bubbling golden top for a hearty and comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g/1lb ground beef or sausage
  • 1 medium onion - chopped
  • 2-3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg to mix with ricotta
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoonblack pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil or Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain.
  2. Brown ground meat with onion and garlic in a pan. Add marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta with egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a baking dish, layer: noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella — repeat layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes until bubbly.
