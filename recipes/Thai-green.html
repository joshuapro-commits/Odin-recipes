Thai green

A coconut-based curry with green curry paste, meat, and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 300g chicken or tofu
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (bell peppers, bamboo shoots)
  • 1 tbsp fish or soy sauce
  • Thai basil (optional)

Steps:

  1. Saut√© curry paste in a little oil (1 min).
  2. Add chicken and stir until coated.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and simmer 10 min.
  4. Add veggies, cook 5 more min.
  5. Season with fish sauce, add basil. Serve with jasmine rice.
